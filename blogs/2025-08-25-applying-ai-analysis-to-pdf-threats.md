In our previous post we extended VirusTotal Code Insights to browser extensions and supply-chain artifacts. A key finding from that analysis was how our AI could apply contextual knowledge to its evaluation. It wasn’t just analyzing code in isolation, it was correlating a package's stated purpose (its name and description) with its actual behavior, flagging malicious logic that contradicted its public description.
