We are excited to announce the launch of Saved Searches in Google Threat Intelligence (GTI) and VirusTotal (VT), a powerful new feature designed to streamline your threat hunting workflows and foster seamless collaboration across your security team. From Campaign to Feature: Better Search Efficiency For the last month, we’ve highlighted the critical importance of mastering search in our ongoing #monthofgoogletisearch campaign. We saw how security teams rely on complex, highly-tuned queries to identify threats, track adversaries, and perform deep-dive investigations.
