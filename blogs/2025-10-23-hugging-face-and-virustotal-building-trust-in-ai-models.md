We’re happy to announce a collaboration with Hugging Face , an open platform that fosters collaboration and transparency in AI, to make security insights more accessible to the community. VirusTotal’s analysis results are now integrated directly into the Hugging Face platform, helping users understand potential risks in model files, datasets, and related artifacts before they download them. Security context where you need it When you browse a file on Hugging Face, you’ll now see security information coming from different scanners, including VirusTotal results.
